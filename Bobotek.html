About Our Company

BOBOTECH INC.


Who we are

Welcome to Bobotech! We are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about software and hardware development.
Bobotech is an IT software and hardware development company based in Kira,Kampala Uganda.
We are invested in the technological growth of the country based in the IT sector. Our mission is to create and build reliable computer technologies for Uganda and the rest of the world as we grow. Also, we aim to decrease greatly the rate of unemployment in the country by availing job opportunities to the youth and enrolling others in our apprentinceship programs to nurture their skills in the field of Information Technogy.

Our History

Founded in 2027, we have been serving our clients with dedication and commitment. Over the years, we have built and put in place our efficient Operating System,LyonOs that operates on our equally efficient Bobolaps. We deal in the
production of software for phones and computers(Operating Systems and Application Systems)
and also production of phones
(Bobotels and tabs) and computers(Bobolaps) themselves. Our products are all top-notch yet
affordable and have been proven to be durable and reliable over time.
